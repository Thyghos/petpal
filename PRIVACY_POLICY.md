# Privacy Policy for Petpal

**Last Updated: March 25, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Petpal ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your pets. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Petpal mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Pet Information**: Name, species, breed, weight, date of birth, and photos
- **Health Records**: Veterinary documents, health history, vaccination records
- **Reminders**: Medication schedules, appointment dates, care notes
- **Emergency Contact Information**: Veterinarian contact details
- **Insurance Information**: Pet insurance details and claims (if provided)

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Location Data**: Approximate location for finding nearby pet-friendly places, veterinarians, and services (only when you use features that request location, such as Travel Mode)
- **Device Information**: Limited device/OS details only as needed for features you use (for example maps and AI APIs do not receive your full pet database)

Apple may provide the developer with **aggregated crash diagnostics and App Store analytics** through App Store Connect (for example crash logs and download statistics). Petpal does not embed third-party advertising or cross-app tracking SDKs in the app.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide core app functionality (pet profiles, reminders, health tracking)
- Generate AI responses for pet care questions
- Find nearby pet-friendly locations
- Store and display your pet's health records
- Send reminder notifications
- Improve app performance and user experience

## Data Storage

### On your devices
- Pet profiles, reminders, health history, emergency QR data, insurance, pet-sitter notes, and attachments are stored in the app database on your device using Apple’s **SwiftData** framework.
- We do not operate our own servers for this app data.

### iCloud sync (CloudKit)
- If you are signed in to **iCloud** on your devices, Petpal can **sync your app database** across your own Apple devices (for example iPhone and iPad) using Apple’s **CloudKit** private database tied to your **Apple ID**.
- Sync uses Apple’s infrastructure; see [Apple’s Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We cannot read your iCloud data on Apple’s servers.
- Sync is **not** instant: allow time and keep the app open occasionally on Wi‑Fi/cellular so changes can upload and download. If iCloud is off or unavailable, the app keeps working with **local** storage on that device only.

### Backup export / import
- You can export selected categories to a **JSON** file or a **ZIP** archive (same content) and share it (Mail, AirDrop, Files, etc.). Anyone with the file can read its contents.
- Import can merge new rows, update existing rows, or replace all data on the device, as you choose in the app.

### Cloud processing (network features)
- AI chat queries are processed by third-party AI providers you configure (or a proxy). Only prompt text you send is transmitted for a response.
- Location and place search may use Apple Maps and third-party APIs when you use those features.

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your pet's health records with anyone
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Track you across other apps or websites

We MAY share anonymous, aggregated data:
- For analytics to improve app functionality
- With service providers who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality)

## Third-Party Services

Petpal may use the following third-party services (depending on features you turn on):
- **Apple iCloud / CloudKit**: Optional sync of your app database between your devices when you use iCloud
- **AI providers (e.g. Anthropic, Google)**: Optional Vet AI when you add API keys or use a configured proxy
- **Apple Maps**: Location-based features on Apple platforms
- **Geoapify** (when configured): Pet-friendly place search

Each service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored in the app
- **Delete**: Remove any or all of your pet profiles and data
- **Export**: Share your data via the app's export features
- **Opt-Out**: Disable location services or AI features
- **Contact Us**: Ask questions about your data

## Data Retention

- Data remains in the app (and in your **iCloud private database** if iCloud sync is active) until you delete it or change it via import
- Deleting the app removes the **local** copy on that device; **iCloud** copies may remain until you remove data in-app, replace via import, or manage storage with your Apple account
- Exported backup files you saved elsewhere are **not** deleted when you delete the app
- AI queries are not stored long-term by us

## Children's Privacy

Petpal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is for pet owners of legal age.

## Security

We take data security seriously:
- Data stored locally on your device is protected by iOS security
- Encryption in transit for network communications
- No unnecessary data collection
- Regular security updates
