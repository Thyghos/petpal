## 💡 Pro Tips for Success

### 1. Don't Rush
Quality over speed. Better to take an extra day to test properly than to get rejected.

### 2. Test on Real Devices
Simulators don't catch everything. Borrow an iPhone if you don't have one.

### 3. Read Everything Carefully
Apple's error messages usually tell you exactly what to fix.

### 4. Keep Good Notes
Document what you do each day. Helps if you need to troubleshoot later.

### 5. Ask for Help Early
Don't spend hours stuck on something. Google it, ask in forums, or contact Apple.

### 6. Stay Positive
Rejections are normal. They help you improve. Don't take them personally.

### 7. Backup Everything
Use Git or Time Machine. Don't lose your work!
